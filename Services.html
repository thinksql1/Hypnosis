About Darrell

I'm a clinical hypnotherapist, musician, and facilitator based in Michigan. My work blends compassionate presence with practical tools: breath, sound, scent, and symbol. Whether in a quiet 1:1 session or a lively community gathering, my aim is simple—help you feel safe, seen, and steady so your own wisdom can lead.

My approach is inclusive and research-aware, drawing from modern psychology, contemplative arts, and accessible ritual forms. Everything is optional, consent-based, and tailored to your comfort and values.

Principles I Work By

  • Safety & Consent: You set the pace; we choose together.
  • Gentle Science: Use what helps; leave what doesn’t.
  • Respect for Faith & Background: Adaptable to Christian, interfaith, or secular settings.
  • Integration: Simple follow-ups you can continue at home.

FAQ

Is this religious?

No. It’s welcoming to everyone—Christian, interfaith, or secular. We focus on breath, attention, and supportive practices you can adapt to your beliefs.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes. Most offerings are available online via private link; group events can be hosted on site in Michigan.

Is incense smoke required?

No. We primarily use gentle warming on electric heaters. Scent is optional, and we can accommodate sensitivities.

Do you provide medical advice?

No. Sessions are educational and supportive—not a replacement for licensed medical, psychological, or pastoral care. I’m happy to collaborate with your providers.
