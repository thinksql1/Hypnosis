Find Your Calm

A simple technique to clear the painful sensations of neuropathy.

1

Get Ready

Sit comfortably with your feet flat on the floor. Keep your hands and arms uncrossed to allow energy to flow freely.

2

Scan, Describe & Rate

Scan your body for uncomfortable sensations. Describe them in a colorful, even childlike way. For example:

  • "It feels like sand is stuck in my shoes."
  • "My nerves are trying to stretch out of my toes."

Then, give the feeling a number from 0 (no discomfort) to 10 (unbearable). This helps track your progress.

3

Breathe & Clear

Take a slow, deep breath in. As you exhale completely, gently say the words:

"Feeling Clear"

4

Wait & Observe

Pause for 15-20 seconds. Allow your breathing to return to its natural, easy pace. Simply be present and notice what happens.

5

Re-Scan & Repeat

Check in with the area you were focusing on. What has changed?

  • Is the sensation less intense? Give it a new number.
  • Did it move or change its quality?
  • "The tingling is gone, and the 'sand' is now a 2."

Repeat the process—from describing the new sensation to breathing and clearing—until you feel the desired relief. A rating of 0 means it's totally clear and resolved. You are in control.

That's it! You have the power to change your sensations.

This is a self-help technique and not a substitute for professional medical advice.
